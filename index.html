I took my tim to watch all of if and what stood out for me was the school of engineering, i was so fascinated with front end, it became my first love, i did my own research using other sources to confirm this passion and it checked out. My research led me to Freecodecamp a free service to learn frontend online and so i begun, till i saw Ruby partner with alt school to discount fees, i took that chance, passed the entrance examinations and here we are. The major thing that made me choose altschool i'd say is the community and the how good students that pass out from there are. So it was an easy decision joining AltSchool Africa and i've loved it ever since.
